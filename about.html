About Rodcroft

A Zimbabwean civil engineering contractor achieving more with less — since 2003.

Who We Are

Established 2003. Built on Experience.

Rodcroft Civil Engineering Contractors is a privately-owned Zimbabwean entity specialising in the provision and construction of civil engineering services. Established and incorporated under the Companies Act (Chapter 24:03) in 2003, the company has grown significantly and built a reputation for quality, client-oriented outcomes.

Our areas of specialisation include roads and transport infrastructure, bulk civil earthworks, dam construction, structural design and construction, sewer reticulation, water systems, storm water drainage, canal construction and irrigation development — and we also offer affordable plant hire. Rodcroft is recognised as a Category A civil engineering contractor by the Construction Industry Federation of Zimbabwe (CIFOZ).
